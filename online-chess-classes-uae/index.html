Chess in UAE: The Vision 2030 Chess Revolution

UAE is experiencing one of the most dramatic chess booms in the world. Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah have all hosted major chess events in recent years, and the Kingdom's ambition to develop a generation of strong players is backed by significant institutional support.
